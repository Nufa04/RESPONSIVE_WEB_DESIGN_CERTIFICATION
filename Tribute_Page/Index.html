Tan Sri P. Ramlee

The Legendary Entertainer of Malaysia

Here's a timeline of P. Ramlee's life:

  • 1929 - Born in Penang, Malaysia as Teuku Zakaria bin Teuku Nyak Puteh on the morning of Eid al-Fitr.
  • 1948 - Discovered by director B.S. Rajhans during a song competition and joined Malay Film Productions at Jalan Ampas, Singapore.
  • 1955 - Directed his very first feature film, 'Penarek Becha', which became a massive success and proved his directorial talent.
  • 1957 - Released 'Pendekar Bujang Lapok', kickstarting the legendary comedy series that remains a cultural masterpiece.
  • 1962 - Won the 'Best Comedy Film' award at the Asia-Pacific Film Festival, proving his influence spanned across Asia.
  • 1973 - Passed away at the young age of 44, leaving behind a timeless legacy of 66 films and over 350 evergreen songs.

"His artistic contributions were a profound reflection of society, beautifully weaving together humor, romance, and deep social messages that remain evergreen today."

-- Cultural Arts Council of Malaysia
